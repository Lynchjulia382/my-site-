Intro

Im currently 16 years old and a design student enrolled in the Eaton RESA program at lansing community college and this is my arts 102 and arts 171 final portfolio. Over the last year I have gained a passion for graphic design and this portfolio is my way of expressing that. I have many hobbies including photoshop,reading, writing, along with graphic design.

You may be wondering what is arts 102 and arts 171? Well arts 102 is a mixture of design and commuication using design concepts and vocabulary and arts 171 is teaching design through a photoshop lens. On this website you will be looking at some of my designs woth photoshop and without photoshop and also some peices I consider some of my best work.

Best

This is one of my favorite personal ones I have done this year, which is why it is in my portfolio. I really love this design because I feel as it is a reflection of myself I always have my head in the clouds, think about the next mountain I will climb in my life. For this project I learned not only double exposure with the image and the model but also how to adjust levels of lights and darks better.

This is my Book cover I did to show different types of effects you can do with text. This image is in my portfolio because I really love the overlapping of layers and the overall composer of this design. While completing this design I learned how to use a gradient fill to really make the text stand out. I also really like this design because I am utterly mesmerized by nature and why not express that using design?

This is my second of two new designs and has a yellow aesthetic. It is included in my portfolio because it has lots of my favorite things,(cameras,books,yellow, and dead end roads that you can explore). For this design I used stroke,opacity, selection tool and overlapping layers to complete it.

Design

This is my rhythm design and probably one of my favorite projects, I remember how challenging it was for me because you didn’t have a base for the design. You were given a word and had to express that word through abstract art and that was the importance of the project opening you mind to the idea of creative art and really letting you creativity free. This design is included in my portfolio because it was my first time really challenging my creativity and liking the outcome.

For this project is was all about experimenting with value using black & white to tell or change a story. This is included in my portfolio because I really enjoyed Changing how this the design is viewed by just adjusting the levels of black & white. Also I really love mystery books and movies and this could be a book cover.
